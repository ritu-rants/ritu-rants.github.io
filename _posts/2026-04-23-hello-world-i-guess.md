So here we are. I started a blog.

I know, I know — it's 2026 and people are supposed to be posting 15-second videos of themselves pointing at floating text. But I've always been better with words than with choreography, and honestly? I missed the internet feeling like a *place* instead of a performance.

## Why "Ritu Rants"?

Because I rant. A lot. Ask anyone who's had the pleasure of sitting next to me during a bad movie, a confusing tech demo, or a particularly infuriating grocery store experience. I have *opinions*, and they need somewhere to live that isn't my group chat at 2 AM.

## What to expect

A little bit of everything:

- Thoughts on tech and the internet
- Life stuff that I need to process by writing about it
- Cultural takes that nobody asked for
- The occasional deep dive into something niche that I've become temporarily obsessed with

No schedule, no editorial calendar, no SEO strategy. Just me, typing.

## Why a blog? Why now?

I wanted a space that's mine. Not rented from an algorithm, not shaped by what gets engagement, not designed to make me "build a brand." Just a quiet-ish corner where I can think out loud.

If you're reading this — welcome. Stick around if you want. I'll be here, ranting.

*— Ritu*
